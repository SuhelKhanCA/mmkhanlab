Dr Md Musawwer Khan

Dr. Md. Musawwer Khan is presently working as an Associate Professor in the Department of Chemistry, at Aligarh Muslim University. He has been awarded his Ph.D. Degree from IIT, Guwahati.


Education:

  1. 1996: 10th JNV, Deoria with I division.
  2. 1998: 10+2, JNV, Gonda with I division.
  3. 2001: B.Sc. (Hons.) in Chemistry with Mathematics and Physics from AMU, Aligarh with I division.
  4. 2003: M.Sc. (Organic Chemistry) from AMU, Aligarh with I division.
  5. 2004: B.Ed. (Physical Science) from AMU, Aligarh with both practical and Theory I division.
  6. 2005: CSIR-JRF (NET) in Chemical Science.
  7. 2011: Ph.D. (Organic Synthesis) from IIT Guwahati.

Schmidt, Department of Chemistry, University of Konstanz, Germany in March 1992. After completion of German Language course (01.07.92 to 31.10.92) at Goethe Institute at Murnau in Germany. Later joined in University of Konstanz on 1.11.1992 and worked on carbohydrate chemistry and in total synthesis of natural products up to 30.12.1994
